Why YAKK Exists

Most Web3 platforms extract. YAKK Studios tracks extraction. Crypto is the greatest wealth-transfer mechanism ever built — and most of that transfer goes the wrong way. Retail holders lose. Coordinated insiders win. The tools to detect and expose this have never existed at the community level.

YAKK Studios builds those tools. Not for institutions. Not for KOLs. For the person who puts £200 into a Solana token and deserves to know if they're buying into a coordinated exit.

Four Founding Principles

Transparency Over Hype
Every claim is backed by on-chain evidence. No promises that can't be verified. The forensics don't negotiate.
Determinism Over Opinion
Gini > 0.85 = High Risk. Always. The system applies consistent logic, not editorial judgment shaped by who's watching.
Community Over Insiders
The 5% tax flows to stakers and holders, not to a founder wallet. The NFT royalty punishes paperhands. Every mechanic points inward, not upward.
Build Over Shill
The platform ships. The KOL outreach doesn't. The work speaks — and it is shipped before it is announced.
